About TriState SportPsych

Our mission is to inspire athletes to connect, enjoy, and achieve mastery by delivering the newest innovations in sport psychology. We partner with athletes (youth to professional) to build tools for lifelong wellness.

Our Approach

We specialize in Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), Mindfulness-Based interventions, and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT).

  • Understanding the culture of high performance
  • Addressing unique stressors (coaches, injuries, pressure)
  • Building authentic, honest relationships

Who we work with

Athletes of all ages (Children 6-10, Teens, Adults), Coaches, Parents, and Performing Artists.

Whether you are a youth athlete or an Olympian, we provide tools for life and sport.
