Common Questions

I've never done therapy. Is it awkward?
It's normal to feel nervous. My job is to make it as comfortable as possible. You don't have to "perform" or have the right words. We'll start where you are, and we'll go at your pace.
What happens in the free consultation?
It's a 15-minute phone call. You can tell me briefly what's going on, and I can explain how I work. If we seem like a good fit, we schedule a first session. If not, I can help point you toward other resources.
Do you see teenagers?
Yes, I work with pre-teens and teenagers, often involving family dynamics to ensure support continues at home.
