About TriState SportPsych

We formed TriState SportPsych with the belief that athletes benefit from a confidential place to explore life inside and outside of sport. We help you build a better athletic life, healthier relationships, and greater purpose.

Our mission

Inspire athletes to connect, enjoy, and achieve by delivering the newest innovations in sport psychology.

  • We understand the pressure of athletic culture
  • We know the unique stressors you face
  • We prioritize honest, authentic relationships

Who we work with

Professional, college, high school, and Olympic athletes, coaches, parents, and performing artists.

Specialized in mindfulness and acceptance-based interventions.
